Please click on the buttons below to take a look into some of my job duties and see what it's like to be an Electronic and Technical Services Librarian!

My job duties here include managing and helping maintain our EZProxy, which allows for remote and campus access to library resources. This is an essential part of my job, especially during COVID!

My job duties here include maintaining our integrated library system which includes our circulation and cataloging module. This also includes all of our ebooks and ejournals. Basically all the print and electronic material come through me!

My job duties here include ensuring the library website is updated and nothing is broken. I have less control here then I would like, but I was able to redesign the entire look and feel of the website which was so much fun! We worked with a local web developer and I think it came out great! Thanks to this class I was able to communicate pretty effectively with them! Here is a snap shot of what it looks like. You can see the full thing here!

My job duties here include providing training to students, faculty, and staff in all areas of electronic resources, like our databases, mobile apps, collections, and discovery system. This is a very fun part of my job!

My job duties here include helping and troubleshooting all the various problems and issues that arise with our resources. These can vary quite a bit, but so far I have been able to help in one way or another!
