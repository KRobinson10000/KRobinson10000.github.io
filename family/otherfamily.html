Randy and Rhonda Shockley

My Wife's Parents

This is Randy and Rhonda Shockley and they are my wife's parents. Unfortunately Randy passed away a few years after my father did of a different type of brain cancer. I'm not sure what the odds of something like that happening are, but it happened. He was a great man, and was even the one that married my wife and I! He was certified to do so in the months before out wedding, and that is an incredibly special memory for my wife and I. I am happy I knew him, but like my father, our time together was cut way to short. We still have Rhonda, and my wife's three sisters!
