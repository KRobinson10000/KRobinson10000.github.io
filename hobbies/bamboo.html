Mini Bamboo Forest

The Story of Our Bamboo

It may sound odd but Bamboo has had a very special place in our family for decades! It started over 25 years ago when my father was first given a single small black bamboo. From there it became something of a passion for him and he grew it into a mini forest that lines an entire side of our property. After my father passed away eight years ago, my mother moved away and my wife and I moved into my childhood home. By proxy we took over care of the bamboo and have caught the “bug” as my father once had it! It’s beautiful, serene, and hearty!
