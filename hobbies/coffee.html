My MoccaMaster coffee maker!

Black Gold!

I have to profess my absolute love of coffee! It may seem odd to have this as a “hobby” but let me tell you, it most certainly is! It started a few years ago when my wife bought me a Nespresso machine and my first French Press. I say “first” because I have many different sizes and brands now haha. From those early days I realized just how good coffee can be and what I had been missing out on all these years! Now I have almost a dozen devices and machines that can make coffee! My latest, and all-time favorite, is my MoccaMaster! It cost way more than I’d like to admit, but man was it worth it! I normally order coffee online, but I decided to try some Pete’s coffee as I have heard it is good! I guess we will soon see!
