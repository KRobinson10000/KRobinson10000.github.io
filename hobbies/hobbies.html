Hobbies

It's a really fun thought experiment to run through what is and isn't a hobby in ones life. I was surprised at the number I came up, especially as I used to think I really had no hobbies at all! I have a pretty neat mix of childhood hobbies that have stuck with me and a number of fairly recent ones that I started being interested in after I was married. It's funny how we grow and change over time, but it is also really special to be able to create something that can showcase it!
