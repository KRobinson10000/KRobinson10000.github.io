Natural Redwood Fence!

We have about 25 Redwoods that create a natural fence along one side of our property and they are beautiful! A couple of them now tower above our house. All together they are between 25 and 30 years old now. It has been really incredible to watch them slowly grow into the behemoths they are now. These, like the bamboo, were a passion originally by my late father. You can read a bit more about that on my bamboo page. There is an entire eco system of birds, rabbits, and squirrels that have created a home in these redwoods. That in itself has been special, but adding on the unique connection to my father has made this one of my favorite pastimes. There is a lot of work that goes into maintaining them and you can see by the picture that there is some browning going on which is literally just from a lack of water. We have been limited in how much water we can use where I live so it has been hard to keep up with them! Also, the smoke has not helped in the least bit. Overall they are a true treasure though!
